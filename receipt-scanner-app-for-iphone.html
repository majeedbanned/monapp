A receipt scanner that fits real iPhone workflows

Many people looking for a receipt scanner app for iPhone are really looking for something broader: a fast way to capture receipts, verify the important fields, and keep those records useful later when the month needs review. Monely is designed around that full workflow instead of stopping at image capture.

That means the app does more than save a photo. It helps turn paper receipts, screenshots, saved images, and PDFs into structured expense data that can feed spending views, reports, subscriptions, and shared ledgers.

Scan receipts, photos, screenshots, and PDFs

Monely supports multiple inputs because real finance records do not come from one source. Some purchases end with a paper slip, others arrive as email PDFs, and some records are just screenshots from another app.

  • Scan paper receipts directly with the iPhone camera.
  • Import photos or screenshots already saved in the photo library.
  • Bring PDF bills and digital receipts into the same capture path.
  • Use manual entry when typing is faster than scanning.

OCR is useful only if the review step stays clear

Receipt OCR is most helpful when it does not force blind trust. Monely reads the document, extracts fields such as merchant, date, total, and currency, and then keeps those values visible before the entry is saved to the ledger.

This is important for anyone who wants to keep cleaner records without spending time retyping everything. The goal is not automation for its own sake. The goal is to reduce friction while still keeping the important numbers reviewable on iPhone.

Who this page is for

This workflow is a good fit for:

  • People who still collect paper receipts and want cleaner monthly records.
  • Freelancers or small operators who mix PDFs, screenshots, and camera scans.
  • Anyone who wants receipt capture tied directly to expense tracking instead of sitting inside a separate scanning app.
  • Users who need local-first behavior and want to decide when AI analysis is actually used.
Monely is not just a receipt image vault. It is an iPhone finance workflow where captured receipts can become searchable, reportable ledger entries.

Privacy and control still matter

Monely is designed around a local-first workflow. Receipt images and related records stay on the device by default, and AI analysis is described as a user-initiated action rather than an invisible background process.
