FAQ

Common questions before you download.

Here’s the short version of what Monely does, who it is for, and where to find support details.

What is Monely?

Monely is an iPhone expense tracker built for fast receipt capture, bill logging, and clearer monthly visibility in one app.

Who is Monely for?

It fits solo tracking, shared household spending, and couples or families who want one calmer place to manage everyday finance.

How do I add receipts and bills?

Use the camera for paper receipts, or import a saved photo, screenshot, or PDF so everything enters the same capture flow on iPhone.

How does Monely help me understand spending?

Monely keeps month-to-date spending, focused reports, and underlying entries close together so you can see what changed without digging around.

Can I use Monely with a partner or family?

Yes. Shared ledgers are built for couples and families that want one shared finance view while keeping the workflow simple.
